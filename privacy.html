Privacy Policy Stock Dashboard is a personal local investment dashboard used only by the account owner. The app uses Gmail readonly access only to read emails labelled depot-import. These emails are used to import portfolio trade confirmations, holdings, cash movements, and depot-related records. The app uses Gmail send access only to send personal action summaries and decision digests to the account owner. The app does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties. Data may be stored locally on the account owner's own computer, including portfolio data, trade confirmations, market data, cached analysis files, and decision-support outputs. The app does not provide public access to other users and is intended for personal, internal, non-commercial use. Contact: michael.d.o.vorbeck@gmail.com
