Terms of Service Stock Dashboard is a personal local investment dashboard intended only for use by the account owner. The app provides portfolio tracking, depot import, market-data organization, and decision-support summaries for personal investment workflow. The app does not provide financial advice to the public. Outputs are informational and must be reviewed by the account owner before any investment decision. The app may use Gmail readonly access to read emails labelled depot-import for portfolio import. The app may use Gmail send access to send personal action summaries to the account owner. The account owner is responsible for verifying imported data, portfolio values, trade records, and any investment decision made outside the app. The app is provided for personal, internal, non-commercial use.
